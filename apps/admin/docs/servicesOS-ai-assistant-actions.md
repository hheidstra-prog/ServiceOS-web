# ServicesOS — AI Assistant: Questions & Actions

A comprehensive overview of questions and actions a user could ask the AI assistant within the ServicesOS platform.

---

## Clients & CRM

- "Which leads haven't I followed up on yet?"
- "How many open opportunities do I have?"
- "When was my last interaction with [client]?"
- "Which clients have been inactive for more than 3 months?"
- "Create a new lead for [name] with email [x]"
- "Move [client] to Active"

## Quotes & Contracts

- "Create a quote for [client] for [service]"
- "Which quotes are still open?"
- "Which quotes are expiring soon?"
- "Send a reminder to [client] about their outstanding quote"
- "What's my quote conversion rate this quarter?"

## Invoicing & Finances

- "Which invoices are unpaid?"
- "Send a payment reminder to [client]"
- "How much revenue did I generate this month?"
- "What's my projected revenue for next month?"
- "Create an invoice for [client] for [amount]"
- "How much BTW/VAT do I owe this quarter?"
- "What are my outstanding receivables?"

## Projects & Tasks

- "Which projects are currently active?"
- "What are my tasks for today?"
- "Which deadlines are coming up this week?"
- "How many hours did I spend on [project] this week?"
- "Add a task to project [x]: [description]"
- "Which project is behind schedule?"

## Scheduling & Calendar

- "What's on my schedule today?"
- "Schedule an introductory call with [client]"
- "When do I have availability this week?"
- "What appointments do I have tomorrow?"

## Communication

- "Draft a follow-up email for [client]"
- "Write a welcome email for new client [name]"
- "Draft a friendly payment reminder"
- "Write a project update for [client]"

## Insights & Reporting

- "How is my business doing this month compared to last month?"
- "Who are my top 5 clients by revenue?"
- "What's my average project value?"
- "How many new leads did I get this month?"
- "What's my average lead-to-active conversion time?"

## Administration & Compliance

- "Does my VAT return for this quarter look correct?"
- "Which invoices are missing required fields?"
- "Export my invoices for my accountant"

## Proactive Suggestions

- "You have 3 leads that haven't been followed up on in 2 weeks"
- "Invoice #123 is 14 days past the payment deadline"
- "[Client] hasn't had a new project in 4 months — time for a check-in?"
- "Your revenue this month is 20% below last month"
